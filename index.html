What is Hidden Heats?

Hidden heats is a thrift shop that consist of items that is peculiarly or never will be available to outlet stores and malls as of today. Hidden heats offers clothing items that are vintage, limited editions, and with collaborations to certain artists, brands, movies, and many more. Just like other trift shops, hidden heats get there items basically from thrifts all over the world, but what make hidden heats unique is we only get items that are authentic, high value, rare, or what the thrift world called "The Great Finds". We dedicate our time and effort to find such unique items, and can be purchased for a cheap and a reasonable price depending on the value and rarity of the item.
